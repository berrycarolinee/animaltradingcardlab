Red Panda

The red panda has an extended wrist bone that works like a thumb and is used to grasp objects.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Weight: 12 - 20 pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 8 years
  • Habitat: Rainy, high-altitude forest

The red panda is a shy and solitary animal that spends most of its life in trees. Red pandas are omnivores with diets consisting of bamboo, fruit, acorns, roots, and eggs. They forage for food during the nighttime and early morning hours. Red pandas are endangered, but can be found in the mountains of Nepal, northern Myanmar and central China.
